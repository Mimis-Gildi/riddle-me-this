Welcome to *Summer*! Of course this is the first on my list. Can you believe the summer is here?
Zoey, 3, welcomes her fourth. And her brother Anton has his 17th. I'm not telling mine. And yours?

Happy *Memorial Day*, our 159th since Logan's General Orders No. 11 on May 5th, 1868.
My battle buddies and I had a tradition of meeting at Arlington in the 2000s.
Not all of us were US Army from Ft. Bragg -- we also picked up some Marines from Camp Lejeune,
fine folks who regularly trained and competed with us on the Army grounds.
Most of these people have done the full tour in the Army and are now volunteering in Ukraine.
I hope you enjoyed the weekend!

2025 Prague Dark Knights hackathon: "You are an American, `rdd13r`,

People who know us offer support for how "selflessly we put it all on Ukraine." And with that comes a need for clarification.
It is not true as stated. Most of our support for Ukraine is indirect -- we support
America's best sons and daughters fighting in Ukraine. It all began with my own US Army military service decades ago.
And I owe my gratitude for my life to a good friend and once a battle buddy, black Alabama native, now late, MSG (Ret.) `klemo.`
I medically retired in 2000, and he completed his 20+ years, fully retiring in 2020. I was born in Soviet Ukraine, but our hero
along with many other Americans in this story met Ukraine in 2015 training the legendary Azov -- a year into the russian invasion of Ukraine.
And yes, the original Azov was nationalist. The russians will tell you so, loud and often.
What they won't tell you is that the 2014 volunteer militia disbanded;
today's 12th Special Forces Azov Brigade is a different unit, vetted under the Leahy Law,
and the US State Department made that finding official in June 2024.footnote:azov-original[{fn-azov-original}]
Today's Azov is the best child of US SFAS.

That year a lot more was happening you probably never heard about. February 2014: katzap Yanukovych runs to Moscow,
FSB officers walk out of the SBU's Kyiv headquarters with the personnel databases, files burning in the courtyard.footnote:sbu-collapse[{fn-sbu-collapse}]
CIA shows up to help rebuild what's left. By 2015, Lt. Gen. Valeriy Kondratiuk -- newly installed head
of Ukrainian military intelligence -- walks into Langley with Russian Northern Fleet schematics and nuclear-submarine material;
the NYT calls this the moment the partnership pivoted from cautious to operational.footnote:kondratiuk[{fn-kondratiuk}]
CIA outpaces MI6 in scale and tempo. By 2024 the NYT is calling it "_one of the C.I.A.'s most important intelligence partnerships_."footnote:cia-partnership[{fn-cia-partnership}]
Best Americans like `klemo` were more Ukrainian than people knew.

Come the 2022 full-scale invasion, my `klemo` barges into my house announcing "I'm saving the world!"
I've done my darndest to try and talk the old goat out of it. No luck! And soon my house is full of old grunts packing duffle bags.
Now, I have always been well-informed. So, I knew that the International Legion Zelenskyy announced is not where I want my vets.
And I knew that they need kit, armor, arms, pay, and logistics -- they need a lot of expensive things we can't offer.
So, Tania, little Anton and I roll up our sleeves, and we go begging all our past customers for any handouts. That didn't work.
We get bandages, blankets, and undies -- good for nothing rags. But then someone offers to match on shares for our startup.
They wanted the company for a long time. We accepted, and that's how we ended up all in --
in return for operational logistics classes 1, 2, 3, 6, and 8.

_I didn't talk about it, and I was also packing to go -- a bad parent! Somehow, our kid knew it._ +
Luckily God and Anton had other plans for us all!

Things finally disconnected for our family as abruptly as they arose. In 2025, all American companies lost the channels -- us included.

He'd dreaded an old-age death in some hospice -- his personal life did not work out; he had no family or children to speak of.

These people are our nation's finest sons and daughters. Our Memorial Day recollections.

By all means our community is not your typical American community.
