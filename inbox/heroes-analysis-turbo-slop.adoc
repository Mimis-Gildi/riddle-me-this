Of the democratic-world-verifiable claims I could actually read: **8 confirmed clean, 1 real
nuance, 2 blocked by access (not by doubt).**

$14.7M–$16M; "$16M" sits at the top of the range.

*Two links to click once yourself:* The Hill QAnon piece and the intellinews KSE piece both 403'd
my fetch. Both claims are otherwise well-corroborated; just confirm the links resolve for a
reader.

Everything else in this scope stands as written. The historical/Western spine is solid and
correctly sourced -- I checked it against the sources rather than against my priors this time,
which is the only version of this that's worth your time.
